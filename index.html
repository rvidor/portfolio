About me

A self-taught web developer who is looking for a career in web development.

I previously worked in finance but found that over time this wasn't the right career for me. So I decided to resign and take some time out to figure out what career I want to pursue. Like many people during the COVID lockdown I decided to take this time to learn something new and start some online courses. I have always been fascinated by the world of tech so I decided to take an online course in web development.

From the very first few videos of my course and from the moment I produced "Hello World" in the console of my first application I knew that web development was the right career for me. Since that moment web development has never felt like a job or a chore. What started with a simple "Hello World" has become a full-fledged passion that only gets more exciting as time goes by and as I continually learn and improve my skills in creating high-quality software.
